Hi, I'm Lucian Marin.

I have a background in electronics engineering and currently doing what I love, designing and building amazing products. I dare to like meticulous design, modern typography, electronic music and strict engineering. I strongly think that design is all about making impossible possible.

2007 Bought the domain and started working on The Journalist.
2008 Started the journal and finished The Journalist with a more modern design.
2009 Worked on Google’s favicon then featured on their official blog.
2010 Started working on Peculiar. After that, I’ve been interviewed and published in Digerati, the Romanian version of The Setup.
2011 Joined a multi-national team of experts who wrote Newscoop 3 Cookbook, a Sourcefabric manual. After that, I’ve been interviewed and published in The Icon Handbook about Peculiar.
2012 Joined InevoLabs, a local startup with global reach where I worked on Lira Media as a front-end engineer.
2013 Started working on Markdawn. Today I'm still trying to figure out how to do publishing right.
2014 Built Sublevel, a beautiful engineered network and Monochrome, a beautiful engineered browser.
  Currently I’m scaling things up and trying to figure out what’s next.

About this site

The site is created using the latest web innovations then statically generated with Jekyll and Sass. It is hosted by Rohost — probably the best hosting provider from Romania.
