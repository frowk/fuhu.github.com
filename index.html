Truths about me

My name is Fu Hu and written as "" in Chinese. Note that "Fu" is my family name, and "Hu" is my given name, because we Chinese people always put our family name first (Note note, "Cong" is _not_ pronounced as /kong/, its pronunciation is /tsong/). As my name tells you, I am from China, which is a knownly large country and has a five-thousand-year-long history. I, of course, love China and I am proud of our culture very much.

I got my bachelor degree in July 2012. And now, I am living in Beijing, the capital city of China. Currently I am working here for hacking Linux kernel.

I am a programmer of Linux and also a fan of free software. I like writing clever programs for fun and enjoy hacking very much. I am a C language lawyer, that is to say, I like C language very much. But I can also program in Bash, Perl, Python, C++, Ruby, Haskell, Java, x86 assembly etc.. I want to become a hacker like Richard Stallman, who doesn't work for money. :-) Now I am also a member of zeuux community which is an organization whose goal is to spread free software.

Besides playing with computers, maths, music, sketch, science fictions, cooking, bikes and travelling are also my favourites. If you share some interests with me, I will be very glad to know you! You can get my e-mail address from the following command:

echo -e '#define cat(c,d) c##.d \n #define mb(a,b) a##@b \n #define n(e,f) e##f \n mb(n(ask,fuhu),cat(gmail,com))' \
| gcc -E -xc - 2>/dev/null |tail -n 1

Please feel free to write to me. I _strongly_ suggest everyone to use e-mails to contact me.

I am often in comp.lang.c, slashdot.org, lkml and github.com. Glad to see you there!
